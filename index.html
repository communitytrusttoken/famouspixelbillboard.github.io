SPACE AND SPACE WAR

BILLBOARD "SPACE" IS A LOCATION WHERE YOUR NFT IS PLACED. EACH OF PIXEL BILLBOARD CHARACTER HAS A UNIQUE COORDINATES EMBEDED IN THEM. SPACES AT THE BILLBOARD WILL THEN BE REPLACED BY YOU PROFILE IMAGE BASED ON THE COORDINATES OF THE PIXEL BILLBOARD CHARACTER/s YOU OWNED.

SPACE WAR - IS WHERE A BUYER TRIES TO EXPAND HIS/HER SPACE BUT ANOTHER BUYER OWNED THE SPACE FOR HIS/HER EXPANSION. THE OTHER BUYER WILL THEN HOLD HIS SPACE AND WILL TRY TO SELL HIS/HER PIXEL CHARACTER/S IN MUCH HIGHER PRICE.
